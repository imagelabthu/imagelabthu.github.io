Introduction to Visual Computing Lab

The researches of Visual Computing Lab mainly lie on 3D imaging, human computer interaction (including human pose estimation, action recognition, hand pose estimation, hand gesture recognition), industrial inspection and video surveillance etc. We have published hundreds of papers on well-known conferences and journals. Visual Computing Lab has been working close with industries to apply the research results to products. Our lab also keeps close collaboration with famous universities (University College London, Imperial College London etc.) and encourages Ph.D. students to attend international conferences and make acamedic visits overseas.

Alumni of our lab are in:

Intel Research、Microsoft Research、Samsung Research、Google、Sony Research、Baidu、Hulu、China Telecom、Start-ups、National Government Departments, etc.
