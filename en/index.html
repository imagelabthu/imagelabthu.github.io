Tsinghua-Rohm Electronic Engineering Hall

ROHM and Tsinghua University have taken advantage of the Comprehensive Agreement on Cooperation Between Industry and Academia they signed in April 2006. It was against the backdrop of these activities that the parties agreed to build the Tsinghua-Rohm Electronic Engineering Hall in September 2008, pledging to pursue an active research and development program from a long-term perspective. Construction of the facility proceeded with the goal of an April 2011 completion.

The Second Gate of Tsinghua University

It was the first main gate of Tsinghua and has always been a prestigious landmark. It is a classic and elegant gate, made from white columns and arch. The words “Tsinghua Garden” written by Na Tong who once served as the grand councilor of state in the Late Qing Dynasty watches on the university.

Grand Auditorium of Tsinghua University

Located at the heart of the oldest area on Tsinghua campus is the imposing auditorium. Construction on what is now one of the favorite buildings on campus began in September 1917 and concluded in March 1920. The auditorium was one of the four major building projects carried out by Tsinghua School, together with Tsinghua Xuetang, the old library, and the gymnasium.

    Introduction to Visual Computing Lab

    The researches of Visual Computing Lab mainly lie on 3D imaging, human computer interaction (including human pose estimation, action recognition, hand pose estimation, hand gesture recognition), industrial inspection and video surveillance etc. We have published hundreds of papers on well-known conferences and journals. Visual Computing Lab has been working close with industries to apply the research results to products. Our lab also keeps close collaboration with famous universities (University College London, Imperial College London etc.) and encourages Ph.D. students to attend international conferences and make acamedic visits overseas.

    Alumni of our lab are in:

    Intel Research、Microsoft Research、Samsung Research、Google、Sony Research、Baidu、Hulu、China Telecom、Start-ups、National Government Departments, etc.
